Who We Are

Oikos Health Care

Founded in 2024 in Mumbai, Oikos Health Care is a women-led pharmaceutical company combining clinical expertise with strategic vision. The name Oikos means House of Health — symbolizing our mission to create a trusted home for healthcare solutions worldwide.

We are committed to providing essential, high-quality, and affordable healthcare products — from critical hospitalization needs to daily nutrition — ensuring safety, efficacy, and accessibility at every stage.
